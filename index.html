Without A Compass

A year ago I was a substantially different person. Like most kids straight out of highschool, I lacked direction. I had no passions, purpose or idea of what I wanted to do with my life and I was very self-centered. After making the decision to take some time off from school and redirect my path, I decided to volunteer for a humanitartian organization called Surfing the Nations; and my life was drastically changed because of it.   For three months I volunteered my time working with at risk youth, feeding the homeless, and carrying out community projects. This experience lead me to discover my life's passion, helping those in need. I believe that everyone has a calling in life and mine is in humanitarian work.I find fullfillment in giving back to communities, lending a helping hand no matter the task and spreading love and joy to those who are in dark places. So, with your help, I'm going to do just that. The next 12 months I will be traveling to Hawaii, Vietnam, Thailand, India and North Korea.
